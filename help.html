    How to use the MyPhysician app:


  • First you have not created your account on the website then you can sign-up.
  • If you are a doctor ,your registration number is mandatory while sign-up.
  • After sign-up you need to fill-up the required information then only your sign-up will be accepted only after the registration number is verified by medical officials.
  • If you have already have your account,log in with username and password.
  • If you are adoctor,use doctor login,if you are a receptionist use receptionist login and if a patient use patient Login.
  • For the doctor,once you have logged in you can add your appointment calender and time slots and also the scheduled opd sessions and after that you will get the list of patients with their appointment slot.
  • For the receptionist,you will get the appointment list of the patients for your doctor along with the name of the respective patients and receptionist can confirm the appointment and check the payment status of the patient.
  • For the patient,while signing-up enter your whole information correctly along with correct address,so the MyPhysician can fetch the nearest doctors for you.
  • Patient must enter the symptoms he/she is suffering from so the the website can fetch the right doctors for your treatment.
  • After you have entered the symtoms you are suffering from,you will receive the nearest doctors with their appointment slots available for you.
  • Select the feasible appointment for you from the selected doctor's appointment slots available.
  • The appointment will be confirmed by the receptionist and patient will get notified about it.
  • The doctor can make themselves self available in case of an emergency so when a patient has an emergency and uses the emergency services and the doctor will be notified so that he/she can reach the door step for the primary aid till the ambulance arrives.
